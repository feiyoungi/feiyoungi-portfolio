Don't Bubble It Up

UX/UI/IX Design / Animation / App Design

Date: April 2024 - June 2024

Role: UX/UI Designer, Animator

Tools: Figma, Figma Plugins (Motion and Jitter), CuteCut Pro

Donâ€™t Bubble It Up is a redesign and rebrand of the original app, DailyBean. The app allows users to log emotions, weather, and activities through a wide array of buttons. Users can interact with their journal entries not only through buttons but also by adding text and images, enabling a simple, noncommittal way to reflect on their experiences. While the original app focused primarily on a green color scheme, my redesign introduced a more vibrant palette to represent the vastness and uniqueness of emotions. I also prioritized personalization, providing new, free, and accessible features that can be acquired through in-app currency instead of hiding them behind paywalls. The main goal was to enhance microinteractions that give users meaningful feedback while adding an element of fun and excitement.

Don't Bubble It Up: The Rebranded App

Styles

I rebranded the app to feel more playful and expressive by playing with bright colors and bubbly fonts. I implemented filled-in iconography to maintain a bold look.
